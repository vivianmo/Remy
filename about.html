Where we started

Remy was founded at Prnceton University in 2016. We were fed up with spending an hour picking an outfit only to have it ruined by some ugly smartwatch. We deserve tech that reflects our personal style. Combined, Vivian and Sreela own over 100 bags so smart bag charms were the natural place for us to turn to.
